Earn a Graduate Certificate in Business Administration from a Top-Ranked University.

Meet Your Career Goals at Northeastern University.

Northeastern University and the D'Amore-McKim School of Business are renowned for their expertise in experiential learning. This short, full-time 8-month program, based in Charlotte, North Carolina is designed to suit the needs of international students. Through this program, you have the opportunity to:

  • Accelerate your career at one of the USA's top business schools.
  • Build relationships with corporate partners.
  • Gain eligibility to apply for work in the US faster.
  • Potential to apply credits to eligible master’s programs at Northeastern University
  • Benefit from the experience and knowledge of lecturers who are industry leaders.
  • Meet Northeastern alumni and create stronger peer relationships.
  • Take advantage of the many career fairs and workshops at Northeastern University.

You will also develop skills in financial decision making, managing a global enterprise, creating sustainable marketing strategies and managing different people and relationships throughout an organization.

Program Overview

Program Overview

  • 8-month program (2 semesters)
  • 5 master's courses / 15 credits
  • No GMAT required. For ELS students, completion of Level 112 is acceptable.
  • Potential to apply credits to eligible master’s programs at Northeastern University
  • Eligible to apply for OPT
  • Full-time, student visa eligible
  • Tution information

Morning classes are dedicated to Structure and Speaking Practice or Language Studies, Vocabulary Enrichment (in levels 101-109) or Skills Enhancement Classes (in Masters levels), and a lesson in the Language Technology Center. Afternoon classes are dedicated to Reading and Writing.

Applications and Admissions

Applications and Admissions

Required application materials include:

  • A submitted online application ( fee waiver cert-crid-9828 )
  • A current resume
  • Professional letter of recommendation - preferably from a past manager
  • Personal statement
  • Transcripts
  • An intake interview can be conducted in lieu of a TOEFL or IELTS. ELS students may submit equivalent ELS English Language scores of 112.

If you want to WORK:You may be eligible to apply for Optional Practical Training (OPT) to work in the U.S. Successful completion of the Graduate Certificate Program qualifies students to apply for OPT. OPT authorizes students to work anywhere in the US for up to 12 months.

F-1 (I-20) International students may be eligible to apply to study in the US and potentially increase their stay with a combination of academic study and Optional Practical Training (OPT) (12 months).
