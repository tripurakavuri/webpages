About Us

ELS India offers unbiased international education counseling and admissions services through internationally educated counselors to help students choose institutions which most closely match their academic qualifications, career ambitions, budget and any other preferences that they may have. Headquartered in Princeton, NJ, ELS has established a strong network of over 1,700 counseling offices in 103 countries and has guided more than a million students aspiring to study abroad.
ELS offers more than 750 university direct entry pathways and maintains over 84 pathway centers around the world with 58 of them located on university campuses.
