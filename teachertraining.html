COMPREHENSIVE CERTIFICATE PROGRAMS: TEFL CERTIFICATION

Certificate in Teaching English as a Foreign Language

The ELS TEFL Certification Program is an intensive, 4-week, 120-hour course that prepares native and non-native English speakers, either with or without prior teaching experience, to teach English both in the United States and overseas. There is a strong focus on learning the fundamentals of grammar and using context to effectively teach grammar.

The course, conducted in partnership with Via Lingua, features workshops on pedagogy, language awareness and teaching skills. The program includes peer-teaching, six hours of observed practicum, and a focus on understanding and communicating grammar. The course offers a unique workshop on working with young learners. The program also includes support for finding a teaching job upon completion of the course.

The program is located in two U.S. locations: St. Petersburg, Florida, and Boston, Massachusetts. COMPREHENSIVE CERTIFICATE PROGRAMS: CELTA CERTIFICATION

Certificate in Teaching English as a Foreign Language

The ELS CELTA Certification Program is an intensive, 4-week, 120-hour course that prepares individuals with no prior teaching experience to teach in either an ESL or EFL (overseas) setting. It is ideal for those either starting their careers or with some teaching experience, looking for enrichment through a teaching credential.

The course, moderated by Cambridge English, features workshops on pedagogy, language awareness and teaching skills, with six hours of observed practicum and written assignments. The program focuses on Professional Development skills with an emphasis on working overseas, and also includes support for finding a teaching job upon completion of the course.

The program is located in Tacoma, Washington, near Seattle, on the campus of Bates Technical College. Candidates interested in taking the CELTA Certification course must be at least 20 years old, have graduated from high school or have the equivalent qualification and, if a non-native English speaker, have an IELTS™ score of 6.5 or a TOEFL® score of 79.
